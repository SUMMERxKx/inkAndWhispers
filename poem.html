Silent Echoes

March 15, 2024 Nature

In the quiet of the night,
Where shadows dance with light,
Words float like gentle whispers,
Through the stillness of the air.

The moon casts silver beams,
Upon the sleeping earth,
While dreams weave stories,
Of beauty and rebirth.

Time stands still in moments,
When nature holds its breath,
And poetry flows freely,
Like waves upon the depth.
